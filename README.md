# A Harmonized Icon Pack for Android TV Launchers

Built for enthusiasts who love a clean setup, this icon pack takes inspiration from the foundational styling of Mortisshadow’s [Minimal TV Icons](https://github.com/Mortisshadow/minimal-tv-icons) and the structure of SicMundus86’s [ProjectivyIconPack](https://github.com/SicMundus86/ProjectivyIconPack), merging them into a refined, singular aesthetic.

While traditional TV icons feature a chaotic mix of solid shapes, vibrant colors, and mismatched borders, this pack introduces a universal semi-transparent background layer for every single app tile. This subtle transparency softens the UI, letting your background wallpaper breathe while maintaining a crisp, identifiable, and perfectly aligned grid for your apps.

## Highlights:

- Developed specifically for Android TV, Google TV, and custom launcher environments (Projectivy Launcher, FLauncher, Wolf Launcher, etc.).
- Consistent, unified design language that eliminates visual clutter.
- High-contrast, minimalist iconography sitting on a premium translucent canvas.
